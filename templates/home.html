Instructions

How to play ?

  • Read the line from the story page displayed on screen. Page automatically turns if spoken words are correct.
  • Your speech transcript gets displayed in the green box below. Make too many pronunciation mistakes and the box turns red to alert you.
  • Feeling stuck in pronunciation : Just use arrow buttons on the side to turn pages.
  • Want to take a small rest in between : Just pause the game and play when you want.

About and Features

Kidcom is a collection of Interactive Digital stories that makes the activity of reading interesting and exciting to do. But WAITTT… We offer much MOREEE !!!

Improve your Reading Skills

Average accuracy and fluency scores after each story reading session, help you improve your reading abilities.

Be like Shakespeare

Learn new vocabulary and their pronunciation after every story, based on the difficulties that you faced during reading.

Understand your abilities

Analysis and Performance charts to monitor your improvements over a period of time.
